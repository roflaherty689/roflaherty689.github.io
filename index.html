Serbias Trip Through The World Cup

Serbia

For the first 60 years of the world cup (1930 - 1990), Serbia was part of the Socialist Feberal Republic of Yugoslavia, and as such, played with them for it.
The SFR Yugoslavia qualified 8 out of 14 times.

From 1994 to 2006, they played alongside Montenegro, qualifying 2 out of 4 times.

Since then, they've played as an independent country, and made it 3 out of 5 times!
